Privacy Policy – CopyStack

Effective Date: August 2025

CopyStack is a clipboard history manager extension for Google Chrome. It is designed to enhance your copy-paste workflow while prioritizing your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how your data is handled.

Data Collection & Storage

  • CopyStack does not collect or transmit any personal data.
  • All clipboard history is saved using Chrome’s storage.local API and remains on your device.
  • No data is synced, stored externally, or shared with third-party services.
  • Clipboard contents are only recorded when you actively copy text on a webpage.

Permissions Justification

  • activeTab – To detect user-initiated copy events from the active tab.
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) – To allow the extension to work on any site the user copies content from.
  • storage – To store clipboard history locally and persist it across browser sessions.

Monetization

CopyStack offers a Pro version that unlocks additional features through a one-time license key. Payment is handled via third-party platforms (e.g., Gumroad or direct communication) and does not occur inside the extension. The extension does not store, collect, or process payment details.

Security

We do not use cookies, analytics, trackers, or cloud sync mechanisms. All data stays fully local. Users can delete their clipboard history at any time using the "Clear All" feature in the extension popup.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
